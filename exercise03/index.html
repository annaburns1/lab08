All About James Naismith

James Naismith was born on November 6, 1861 in Ontario, Canada. He is best known as the "Father of Basketball" because, well, he is! He invented basketball in 1891 while teaching P.E. at the YMCA. A few years later, he arrived at The University of Kansas (the best university in the world, some might say), where he was the athletic director and basketball coach. Naismith passed away on November 28, 1939, but his legacy lives on forever!

DID YOU KNOW?

James Naismith is the only KU Men's Basketball coach with a losing record.
